SCI Tour & Travels

A school tour refers to a guided visit or an exploration of a school campus, typically for prospective students and their families. School tours are often organized to help individuals or groups get a better sense of the school's environment, facilities, culture, and educational offerings. These tours are especially common for new students, parents considering enrolling their children, or other stakeholders such as prospective staff or educational partners.
