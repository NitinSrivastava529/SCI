Biometric Integration

A biometric device is a piece of hardware used to capture, process, and authenticate biometric data, which is then used to verify the identity of an individual. Biometric devices rely on unique physical traits (such as fingerprints, face, iris, voice, or even palm prints) for identification or authentication. These devices are widely used for security purposes, access control, and user authentication.

WhatsApp Integrations

WhatsApp Integration refers to the process of incorporating WhatsApp messaging features into your application, website, or business process. This can be used for customer support, notifications, marketing, or any communication directly via WhatsApp.

Smart class

A smart class is an educational environment that integrates technology into the teaching and learning process to enhance engagement, collaboration, and efficiency. These classrooms are equipped with advanced digital tools and resources that provide interactive, multimedia-based lessons and facilitate communication between students and teachers. The goal of a smart classroom is to create a more dynamic, interactive, and personalized learning experience.
