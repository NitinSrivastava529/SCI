About Student Care India

Studentcareindia established in 2015, has emerged as a leading provider of school management software solutions in India. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the way educational institutions operate by introducing innovative technology solutions


School ManagementSoftwarerefers to a suite of software tools or a system that schools use to manage various administrative, academic, and operational functions. These systems are designed to help schools improve efficiency, reduce paperwork, and streamline communication between teachers, students, parents, and administrative staff. They can be cloud-based (SaaS) or hosted locally.
